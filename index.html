STRATEGY & TACTICS

PHASED APPROACH

EARLY GAME (Turns 1-3)
  • Form defensive line with knights
  • Position archers on high ground
  • Keep mage behind frontline
  • Use forests for cover advantage
MID GAME (Turns 4-8)
  • Flank isolated enemies
  • Focus fire on high-threat targets
  • Use berserkers to break formations
  • Maintain unit cohesion for morale
LATE GAME (Turns 9+)
  • Eliminate enemy leaders first
  • Protect injured units at all costs
  • Use terrain chokepoints
  • Don't overextend - consolidate gains
