LEVEL STRATEGIES

Level 1: Training Grounds
  • Learn basic movement and attack
  • Use forest for cover
  • Keep mage behind frontline
Level 2: Darkwood Approach
  • Dense forest limits vision
  • Archers excel here
  • Watch for ambushes
Level 3: Forest Outpost
  • River crossing at two points
  • Enemy archers cover bridges
  • Split force or concentrate
Level 4: Mountain Pass
  • Narrow path - funnel enemies
  • Mountains give accuracy bonus
  • Take high ground
Level 5: Mountain Fortress
  • First boss encounter
  • Clear weaker units before focusing on the boss
  • Use mountain peaks for archers
Level 6: Gremlin Swarm
  • Lots of pesky gremlins
  • Gremlins are weak but fast and soo anoying
  • Area control is key
Level 7: Misty Lowlands
  • Mixed terrain transition
  • Mountains behind, forest ahead
  • Scout carefully
Level 8: Swamp of Sorrows
  • Movement HALVED each turn
  • Gremlins move normally
  • Plan moves 2 turns ahead
Level 9: Fog of War
  • Vision only 3 tiles
  • Archers outrange vision (5 tiles)
  • Enemies appear from fog
  • Move cautiously as a group
Level 10: The Final Stand
  • 4-tile bridge crossing under fire
  • At least three bosses in large village
  • Focus one boss at a time
  • Use stone bridge for cover
