üß† BATTLE TIPS

    Positioning is Key: Use forests for cover, avoid water (blocks movement)
    Protect your Mages: They're fragile but can heal your frontline!
    Manage Morale: Keep units together - nearby allies boost morale recovery!
    Focus Fire: Eliminate high-threat enemies first (Mages ‚Üí Archers ‚Üí Berserkers ‚Üí Knights)
    Use Terrain: Mountains give a defense bonus, but reduce accuracy
    Water: It is not possible to pass through water. 

üèÜ PROGRESSION SYSTEM

Complete 3 EPIC LEVELS:

    Defend the Village - Training Ground
    Forest pursuit across the river - Face tougher enemies
    Mountain Fortress - Final boss battle! 

Surviving units carry over between levels (with partial healing). 
The fallen are lost forever!

Eliminate all enemy units to win and move to the next level
If all your units are defeated or have fled, you lose.
Leveling Up

Units gain experience (XP) by participating in combat:

    Hit an enemy: 15-23 XP
    Kill an enemy: 40-56 XP
    Heal an ally: 8-12 XP

XP Required per Level 

    Level 1 ‚Üí 2: 100 XP

    Level 3 ‚Üí 4: 225 XP (475 total)
